About PromoHub

PromoHub is a 100% free, automated discovery platform. Every few hours, our system scans the web for the best deals, product launches, tools, and stories — then publishes them here for you.

How it works

This site is built using completely free tools:

  • GitHub Pages hosts the site (free, unlimited bandwidth tier available)
  • GitHub Actions runs the automation every few hours (2,000 free minutes/month)
  • RSS feeds from across the web supply fresh content
  • Optional AI rewrites headlines for clarity and engagement

Affiliate Disclosure

Some links on PromoHub are affiliate links. This means if you click a link and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission — at no extra cost to you. This is how we keep the site free and continue curating great content.

We only promote things we believe are genuinely valuable. Affiliate status never influences what we feature.
